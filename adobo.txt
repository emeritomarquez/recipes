Ingredients:
 - 1 clove garlic
 - 10 tbsp soy sauce
 - 2 tsp salt
 - 10 tbsp vinegar
 - 10 pcs chicken breasts and legs
 - 2 tsp black pepper
 - 1 onion

Directions:
 1. Marinate the chicken overnight with garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper and salt.

 2. Saute some garlic and onions.

 3. Add the chicken breasts and legs.

 4. Cook for thirty minutes with the pan cover closed.
